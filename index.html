Tic tac toe

How to play?

Tic tac toe, Noughts and Crosses, Xs and Ox... Is a game for two players (or one planer and the AI) who take turns marking spaces in the board, with X or O. The player who succeeds in marking three squares in a horizontal, vertical, or diagonal row is the winner.

Unfortunately, there is no standard in which refer to who make the first move. To the purpouse of this game, the first turn is gonna be aleatory assigned for one of the player (including A.I if the player chooses to play against it.

.To make a move we just have to click in the square we want to put our mark in, and that's it!
